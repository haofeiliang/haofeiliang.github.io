I am an Eng.D. candidate in Computer Science and Technology at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Yu Yu. My research focuses on **fully homomorphic encryption (FHE)**, with particular emphasis on the TFHE scheme family, including its applications, the design of efficient FHE schemes, and performance optimization of FHE-based protocols.
